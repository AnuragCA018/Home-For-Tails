Adoption Steps

1. Browse our Available Pets and choose the one you want to meet.

2. Contact us using the Contact form or call to request a meet & greet.

3. Fill a short adoption application we check references and suitability.

4. Visit and meet your chosen pet; if all goes well, complete the adoption paperwork.

5. Take home your new family member. We provide basic care tips and follow up.

Helpful Tips

  • Prepare your home: safe area, toys, food, and bed.
  • Be patient new animals need time to settle in.
  • Follow basic vet care: vaccinations, deworming, and microchipping.

Home for Tails
